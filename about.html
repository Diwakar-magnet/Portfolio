About Me

I am Diwakar Gupta. I am a UI / UX designer, web developer as well as a photographer based in Dhangadhi, Nepal. I have extensive experience in web designing; UI/UX & development with great ability of revolve ideas into design which communicates in a creative way. Each design created inspires & helps to achieve my goals as well as customer needs. I have professional experience in design arena accumulated by working with many esteemed companies where I have worked on different projects which helped me to grow & build an identity as a good designer in the industry over the years. My work process involves right from visualizing how customers business can be reflected through the designs to landing the business perfectly with great execution and support. While working for a long time in this industry, I have mastered myself to design websites and photoediting perfect artworks which are off course loved and appreciated by customers. These extensive principles of my design have always helped me to build extensive client network throughout the globe. I am graduate and perceived designing certifications. Also enhancing knowledge and building designing capabilities through day-to-day project experiences. “Coming from an developer background, and not falling in the 9-6 trap, I went on to study & make edited photographs. This landed me in Dhangadhi, and I got to work with self. This taught me to be independent and take my own decisions, and keep pursuing what I love. It is somewhere in between guiding students, taking photowalks & making portraits of friends, I found my calling for Maternity Photography and Potrait photography I photographed my first Potrait in Dhangadhi, the second one in India & thereafter a Destination Matirnity in Dhangadhi in 2022, and it has been a fabulous journey ever since! Having Photographed in more than a dozen countries, and gotten an opportunity to travel to even more such fabulous places. In love with making beautiful films & photographs.
