about us

we will give a very special celebration for you

To always create the most magical and memorable of days for my couples and their guests.

Hello and welcome! I’m Diwakar Gupta. I pride myself on being a highly recommended luxury event planner specialising in overseas and Nepal destination event planning. I am passionate about using my experience and expertise to design and deliver your dream event functions. I dedicate myself to your once-in-a-lifetime celebration with meticulous attention to detail, creative flair and unprecedented industry connections here and abroad. My service is highly professional, but it is also completely bespoke and personal – a hug, not a handshake. I make sure the journey from now until your spectacular event is memorable, enjoyable, and completely stress-free. We’ll have many opportunities to pour over the details in-person, both locally and during our trips together to see vendors and event venues. As the details begin to take shape and the excitement builds, I enjoy being the calm, exacting, and unfailing pillar you trust to see it through to flawless execution.
